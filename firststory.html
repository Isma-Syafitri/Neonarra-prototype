Gunung Kaba'

In Singkawang Malay language, Kaba' means unfinished or imperfect. This story comes from the outskirts of Singkawang, specifically Sei Naram which is close to Bukit Batu.

It started with the ambition of an arrogant young man who wanted to marry a girl from a neighboring village, and ended up becoming a place called Gunung Kaba'. What is their story? Let's take a look!

Click the audio below to listen to the story

Once upon a time, there lived a daughter of a village chief. Her name was Melina.

Melina was very beautiful. She was also polite and kind.

Melina's good personality and beauty made her popular in her village.

Translation

Pada zaman dahulu kala, hiduplah seorang anak perempuan dari kepala desa. Namanya Melina.

Melina cantik sekali. Dia juga sopan dan baik hati.

Kepribadian Melina yang baik dan kecantikannya membuatnya populer di desanya.

Vocabulary
village chief;
polite;
personality;

On the other side, there lived a young man named Wak Ali. He was the son of the neighboring village chief.

However, he was disliked by people because he was arrogant and vicious.

Translation

Di sisi lain hiduplah seorang pemuda bernama Wak Ali. Dia adalah putra dari kepala desa tetangga

Namun, dia tidak disukai orang karena dia sombong dan kejam.

Vocabulary
neighboring;
arrogant;
vicious;

One day, Wak Ali saw Melina for the first time. The young man was stunned by her beauty.

Soon, Wak Ali came to Melina's house to propose to her. But, Melina's father gently rejected the proposal because he knew his lousy personality.

Translation

Suatu hari, Wak Ali melihat Melina untuk pertama kalinya. Pemuda itu terpesona oleh kecantikannya.

Tak lama kemudian, dia datang ke rumah Melina untuk melamarnya. Namun, lamaran tersebut ditolak dengan halus oleh ayah Melina karena mengetahui sifat buruk Wak Ali.

Vocabulary
propose;
reject;
lousy;

Getting rejected, Wak Ali was mad. He planned a bad deed against Melina by creating a poisonous fruit.

Then he told his genies to transform into a bird. After that, he tricked Melina to follow the bird and eat the poisonous fruit.

Translation

Ditolak, Wak Ali Marah. Ia pun merencanakan perbuatan buruk terhadap Melina dengan menciptakan buah beracun.

Kemudian, dia menyuruh jin-jinnya itu berubah menjadi seekor burung. Setelah itu, ia mengelabui Melina agar mengikuti burung itu dan memakan buah beracun tersebut.

Vocabulary
deed;
genie;
poisonous;

After Melina ate the fruit, her head felt dizzy. Soon, she went unconscious. Her friends were panicked.

They screamed for help until some villagers came and helped to evacuate Melina to her house.

Translation

Setelah Melina memakan buah tersebut, kepalanya terasa pusing. Tak lama, dia pun tidak sadarkan diri. Teman-temannya panik.

Mereka berteriak minta tolong hingga beberapa warga datang dan membantu mengevakuasi Melina ke rumahnya.

Vocabulary
dizzy;
unconscious;
scream;

For days, nobody could bring Melina's consciousness back. One of the physicians suggested Melina's father hold a competition with a promise:

"If a man can cure Melina, he will be her husband.

Meanwhile, if a woman can cure Melina, she will be her sister."

Melina's father accepted the suggestion.

Translation

Selama berhari-hari, tidak ada yang bisa mengembalikan kesadaran Melina.

Salah satu tabib menyarankan ayah Melina mengadakan kompetisi dengan janji:

“Jika seorang pria bisa menyembuhkan Melina, dia akan menjadi suaminya.

Sedangkan jika seorang wanita bisa menyembuhkan Melina, dia akan menjadi saudarinya.”

Ayah Melina menerima saran tersebut.

Vocabulary
consciousness;
physician;
suggestion;

Months later, many physicians tried to cure Melina, but nobody succeeded.

Until one day, Wak Ali participated in the competition. Strangely, he succeeded to cure Melina by using a secret magic potion.

Translation

Beberapa bulan kemudian, banyak dokter yang mencoba menyembuhkan Melina, namun tidak ada yang berhasil.

Hingga pada suatu saat, Wak Ali mengikuti lomba tersebut. Anehnya, dia berhasil dengan menggunakan obat rahasia ajaib.

Vocabulary
succeed;
participate;
strange;

Melina's father was unwilling to let his daughter marry Wak Ali.

But, he could not break his promise when he held the competition.

No matter what reason, he could not deny that Wak Ali saved Melina's life.

Translation

Ayah Melina enggan membiarkan putrinya menikah dengan Wak Ali. Namun, ia tak bisa mengingkari janjinya saat menggelar kompetisi tersebut. Apapun alasannya, ia tak bisa memungkiri bahwa Wak Ali menyelamatkan nyawa Melina.

Vocabulary
unwilling;
promise;
reason;
deny;

A few days before the marriage, Melina asked Wak Ali to show evidence of his love for her. Confidently, the young man promised to make a giant mountain for her.

Melina accepted Wak Ali's promise and told him that the marriage plan would be canceled if the mountain did not finish in one night.

Translation

Beberapa hari sebelum menikah, Melina meminta Wak Ali menunjukkan bukti cintanya. Dengan penuh percaya diri, pemuda itu berjanji akan membuatkan gunung besar untuknya.

Melina menerima janji Wak Ali dan memberitahunya bahwa rencana pernikahan akan batal jika gunung itu tidak selesai dalam satu malam.

Vocabulary
evidence;
giant;
marriage;

The process of making the mountain was held almost smoothly soon after that. Wak Ali was confident because he made the mountain with the help of genies.

All of his genies had worked hard since the sunset had disappeared.

Translation

Proses pembuatan gunung berlangsung hampir lancar segera setelah itu. Wak Ali percaya diri karena dia membuat gunung itu dengan bantuan jin. Semua jinnya telah bekerja keras sejak matahari terbenam menghilang.

Vocabulary
almost;
confident;
dissappear;

The smooth process made Melina feel nervous. She did not want to be Wak Ali's wife. So, she needed to make sure the mountain never finished.

Translation

Proses yang lancar membuat Melina merasa gugup. Dia tidak mau menjadi istri Wak Ali. Jadi, dia perlu memastikan gunung itu tidak pernah selesai.

Vocabulary
smooth;
nervous;
never;

Soon, an old lady approached Melina with a mirror and jar. The old lady intended to help Melina and guided Melina to make Wak Ali fail to finish the mountain.

Translation

Tak lama kemudian, seorang wanita tua menghampiri Melina dengan membawa cermin dan guci. Wanita tua itu berniat membantu Melina dan membimbing Melina untuk membuat Wak Ali gagal menyelesaikan gunung tersebut.

Mereka berteriak minta tolong hingga beberapa warga datang dan membantu mengevakuasi Melina ke rumahnya.

Vocabulary
approach;
jar;
intend;

Following the old lady's instruction, Melina lit a torch near the unfinished mountain. After that, she moved the mirror to imitate the sunrise.

Translation

Mengikuti instruksi wanita tua itu, Melina menyalakan obor di dekat gunung yang belum selesai dibangun. Setelah itu, ia memindahkan cermin untuk meniru matahari terbit.

Mereka berteriak minta tolong hingga beberapa warga datang dan membantu mengevakuasi Melina ke rumahnya.

Vocabulary
torch;
mirror;
imitate;

The genies were panicked when they saw the fake sunrise. Immediately, they were scattered to find hiding places.

Melina opened the jar and left it in an empty field to trap the genies. Soon, the jar was full of genies who tried to hide. Then, Melina closed the jar.

Translation

Para jin panik saat melihat matahari terbit yang palsu. Mereka pun langsung berpencar untuk mencari tempat persembunyian. Melina membuka toples dan meninggalkannya di lapangan kosong untuk menjebak jin. Tak lama kemudian, toples itu penuh dengan jin yang berusaha bersembunyi. Lalu Melina menutup toplesnya.

Vocabulary
fake;
empty;
hide;

Wak Ali was furious when he noticed Melina's actions toward his genies. He grabbed the jar and forcefully opened it.

Wak Ali did not know that Melina was the only one who could open the jar.

Translation

Wak Ali menjadi sangat marah saat melihat perbuatan Melina terhadap jinnya. Dia mengambil toples itu dan membukanya dengan paksa. Wak Ali tidak tahu kalau hanya Melina yang bisa membuka toples itu.

Vocabulary
furious;
toward;
grab;

Suddenly, a light emerged from the jar and sucked Wak Ali into it. In his last moment, Wak Ali cursed Melina into a snake that should keep his unfinished mountain.

Melina was not strong enough to handle the jar with her hands. In a blink, the jar shattered into pieces.

Translation

Tiba-tiba muncul cahaya dari guci dan menghisap Wak Ali ke dalamnya. Di saat-saat terakhirnya, Wak Ali mengutuk Melina menjadi seekor ular yang seharusnya menjaga gunungnya yang belum selesai.

Melina tidak cukup kuat untuk memegang guci itu dengan tangannya. Dalam sekejap, guci itu hancur berkeping-keping.

Vocabulary
emerge;
curse;
shatter;

When Melina opened her eyes, she was already a snake. She screams to find her parents.

Melina's parents were so sad when they saw their daughter. The only thing that they could do was pray to God to bless their daughter with patience.

Translation

Saat Melina membuka matanya, dia sudah menjadi ular. Dia berteriak mencari orang tuanya. Orang tua Melina begitu sedih saat melihat putri mereka.

Satu-satunya hal yang bisa mereka lakukan adalah berdoa kepada Tuhan untuk memberkati putri mereka dengan kesabaran.

Vocabulary
parents;
pray;
patience;

Although it was hard, Melina gradually accepted her fate. At least, she was more willing to be a snake rather than Wak Ali's wife.

The curse was the cost that she should paid for her action. She realized that she should not avenge someone's evilness.

Translation

Meski berat, Melina perlahan menerima nasibnya. Setidaknya, dia lebih rela menjadi ular dibandingkan istri Wak Ali.

Kutukan itu adalah harga yang harus dia bayar atas tindakannya. Dia menyadari bahwa dia tidak seharusnya membalas dendam pada kejahatan seseorang.

Vocabulary
gradual;
fate;
avenge;

From that day, Melina sometimes appeared around the mountain to meet her parents in the evening. In the end, the unfinished mountain was called Kaba' Mountain, which means the mountain was never completed in the local language.

Translation

Sejak saat itu, Melina terkadang muncul di sekitar gunung untuk menemui orang tuanya di malam hari. Pada akhirnya gunung yang belum selesai itu dinamakan Gunung Kaba' yang dalam bahasa setempat berarti gunung itu tidak pernah selesai.

Mereka berteriak minta tolong hingga beberapa warga datang dan membantu mengevakuasi Melina ke rumahnya.

Vocabulary
appear;
unfinished;
language;
